# SSC Exams: English Language Study Guide

This comprehensive guide takes you from beginner to advanced in English for SSC.

## 1. Grammar Rules

*   **Subject-Verb Agreement:** The verb must agree with its subject in number and person.
    *   *Rule:* Singular subjects take singular verbs, plural subjects take plural verbs.
    *   *Example:* He **plays** cricket. They **play** cricket.
*   **Tenses:** Past, Present, Future.
    *   *Rule:* Use Present Perfect for actions started in the past and continuing into the present.
    *   *Example:* I have lived here for 5 years. (Not: I am living here for 5 years).
*   **Prepositions:** Show relationships in time and space.
    *   *Example:* I will meet you **at** 5 PM **on** Monday **in** July.

## 2. Vocabulary & Synonyms

Building vocabulary is crucial for synonyms, antonyms, and fill-in-the-blanks.

## 4. Reading Comprehension

*   **Tips for Beginners:**
    1.  Read the questions first so you know what to look for.
    2.  Skim the passage for the main idea.
    3.  Don't assume any outside knowledge; base answers *only* on the passage.
*   **Example:**
    *   *Passage:* The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The dog woke up and barked.
    *   *Question:* What did the dog do?
    *   *Answer:* Barked.
